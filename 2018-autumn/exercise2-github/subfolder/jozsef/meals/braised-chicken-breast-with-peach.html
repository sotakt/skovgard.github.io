Braised Chicken Breast with Milk

This meal is one of my favorite meals and I can assure you that it’ll become one of yours too. The flavours work so well together that you will want to cook it every other day. On top the sensational taste it is actually healthy due to the chicken breast and the greens.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Oil
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Milk
  • Vegetables (to taste)

Making Your Braised Chicken

  1. Heat a pan with some oil, when it’s warm place the cleaned chicken breast and fry both side for 2-3 minutes on medium heat.
  2. Remove the chicken and add some more oil, fry chopped onions and smashed garlic until translucent. Remove pan from the heat.
  3. Slowly pour warm milk into the pan add the vegetables you like and place the chicken into it. Cover the pan and put onto medium heat.
  4. Steam the chicken until cooked through.
    5. Optional

    Serve with rice and salad, optionally add sour cream on top.
