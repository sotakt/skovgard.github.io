Hummus Chicken Tortilla

You can plate it and eat at home or just pack them and take them for lunch at school. You can play with the filling and ingredients to match exactly your taste and needs. This is a really easy recipie and doesn’t take much time to make.

Ingredients

    For Tortillas
  • Flour
  • Water
  • Salt
    • For Filling
  • Hummus
  • Veggies
  • Chicken Breasts
  • Spices
  • Sour Cream
  • Cheese
  • Oil

Making Your Tortillas

  1. Mix some flour and a pinch of salt together.
  2. Warm up a cup of water and put a teaspoon of salt into it.
  3. Pour the warm water into the flour in small portions and mix it continuously until you get a soft, a little sticky dough.
  4. Warm up a dry pan without oil.
  5. Make small balls from the dough and with a rolling pin flatten them out until they start to become translucent.
  6. Put the flattened tortillas into the hot pan. After it “bubbles up” turn it. After 5-15 seconds the tortilla is cooked.
  7. Stack them on top of eachother and cover them with aluminum foil so they steam and remain soft.

Filling Your Tortillas

  1. Place the cleaned, spiced and cut chicken breasts into a hot and oiled pan. Stir them regularly and cover them with a lid.
  2. When they are tender remove them from the heat and let them cool on a paper towel.
  3. Spread some hummus on a tortilla then fill with some vegetables.
  4. Put the chicken breast pieces in and spread some sour cream on top. Season for taste and top the whole thing with cheese.
  5. Roll up the tortilla folding in the ends so the filling stays inside.
    6. Optional

    Slightly brush the rolls with oil and put them into a sandwich maker so it will have some grill marks and the cheese melts inside the rolls.
