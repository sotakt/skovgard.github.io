DANISH RICE PORRIDGE

2 persons
preparation time: 40 min

1 liter

2.25 dl porridge rice
1 dl water
1 liter milk
1 teaspoon salt

Cinnamon sugar

4 tablespoons sugar
1 tablespoon cinnamon

Steps

1. Pour water and rise in a pot and let it boil for about 2 minutes.
2. Add the milk and bring the cream to the boil while stirring.
3. Boil the porridge under low heat. Stir it evenly so that the milk does not burn on the bottom.
The porridge is finished when it has boiled for about 35 minutes.
4. Taste the porridge with a little salt and serve the rice porridge with cinnamon, sugar and a lump of butter.
